# Privacy Policy for TaxNG Advisor

Last updated: December 22, 2025

TaxNG Advisor ("we", "our", "us") is a tax calculator and guidance app designed to help users comply with Nigeria Tax Act 2025. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, how it is secured, and the choices you have.

## Data We Collect
- Account information: name, email (when creating an account or contacting support)
- Tax calculation inputs: financial and business details entered by you (e.g., turnover, expenses)
- App usage data: anonymized usage metrics to improve features
- Device information: device model, OS version (for troubleshooting)

We do not collect sensitive personal data beyond what is necessary for calculations and support.

## How We Use Data
- Provide tax calculations (VAT, CIT, PIT, Payroll, WHT, Stamp Duty)
- Generate payment links and receipts via integrated payment gateways
- Improve app functionality and reliability
- Respond to support requests

## Data Storage and Security
- Local storage: Inputs are stored on your device for convenience.
- Cloud backup (optional): If enabled, backups are encrypted using industry-standard encryption.
- Secure communications: Data exchanged with services uses HTTPS.

## Sharing of Data
We do not sell your data. We only share data:
- With payment gateways to process transactions you initiate
- With cloud backup providers (if you enable backups)
- As required by law or to protect our rights

## Your Choices
- You can delete local data via app Settings.
- You can request account deletion and cloud backup removal by contacting support.
- You may opt out of analytics where offered.
